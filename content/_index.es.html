Cadex's Top Features

Sustainably Proof of Stake

Cadex runs on Proof of Stake, which consumes a fraction of the energy of a Proof of Work network.

Network scalability

Testing shows current capacity at 260+ transactions per second, alongside active research into layer 2 scaling solutions.

Low cost global payments

Send money cheaply, anywhere in the world, without needing to go through a bank.

Privacy

Privacy has always been a central part of Cadex, from the original NavTech to the upcoming improvements.

Quick payments

Transactions show up in seconds, and are confirmed within 30 seconds, making it ideal for business.

Pay a name, not a number

Cadex uses OpenAlias to make sending money as easy as typing an email address.

Values That Are Built Into The Network

  • Open & transparent governance

    No one is in control of Cadex - everyone is free to contribute however they want. There are no forced changes, and every wallet gets a say in the direction of Cadex.

  • Open source everything

    Open source is the backbone of Cadex. Every line of code is viewable, and anyone can contribute to the website. Trust has to be built through transparency.

  • Be your own bank

    Being a peer to peer network means that there are no banks or intermediaries getting in the way. An economic system where your vote counts.

  • Building a new system

    Cadex is symbol of change for how the entire global economic system works - one that returns freedom, privacy, and financial power back to the individual.

  • Fair, clean & green

    No one lives in a bubble - we all affect the world in different ways. This is why Cadex strives to be energy efficient to do right by our planet.

  • Privacy

    Privacy is about giving you a choice. You get to take back control of your personal information, along with who gets access to it.
